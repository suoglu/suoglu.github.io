Scuba diving was a long-delayed passion of my mother. In the summer of 2010, we got our open water certificates together; and I was introduced to the fun and the beauty of the underwater. In 2014, again with my mother, we got our Advanced Open Water certificates. And in 2016, my sister also joined us. Although I didn't have any chance to dive with her until 2022. Unfortunately, I didn't have any opportunity to dive during the last years of my bachelors and after moving to Germany. But, now I am hoping to continue diving in Germany and many other locations thorough the year!


While diving, I also took some photos. You can find here some of the photographs I took during diving, as well as some of my photos of me with my buddies.
